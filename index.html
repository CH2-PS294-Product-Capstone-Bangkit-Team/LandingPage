About Us

We at StuntCare are determined to create positive changes in stunting prevention. Through our application, we combine technology and scientific knowledge to support the growth of children to become a healthy and strong generation.

StuntCare was born from our desire to present a measurable and effective solution in preventing stunting. We provide information, nutrition guidelines, and support for parents and the community to improve the health of children holistically.

The StuntCare team consists of nutritionists, pediatricians, application developers, and professionals who care about children's health. We unite to provide the best solution for stunting prevention.

Why Choose Us

With a steadfast commitment to your well-being, our team of highly trained healthcare professionals ensures that you receive nothing short of exceptional patient experiences.

Overall Solution for Child Health and Growth

The "StuntCare" app provides a comprehensive solution for child health and growth with features covering growth monitoring, personalized nutritional recommendations, and direct consultation with nutrition experts.

Advanced Technology for Nutritional Analysis

This app utilizes image processing and machine learning technology to calculate the amount of nutrition a child receives from food, offering accurate analyses to support better nutritional decisions.

Direct Consultation with Nutrition Experts

The app allows parents to consult directly with nutrition experts without leaving home, providing easy and quick access to quality health information.
